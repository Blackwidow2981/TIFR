Welcome!

The scope of this project is the visual inspection including quality assurance of the circuit boards and silicon sensors used in the new High-Granularity Calorimeter (HGCAL) of the Compact Muon Solenoid (CMS) Detector using Machine Learning Methods. The Quality Assurance framework has been divided into two stages using ten key parameters as checkpoints for quality control which were defined in consultation with researchers at the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR) in collaboration with CERN.
